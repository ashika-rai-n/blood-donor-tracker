Eligibility Checklist (Must Meet ALL)

Before you donate, ensure you meet the essential requirements. Your safety and the recipient's safety are paramount. All criteria are checked at the donation center.

Age & Weight

Age 18 to 65 years | Min. 45 kg (110 lbs)

General Health

Must be healthy, feel well, and free from cold/flu symptoms.

Hemoglobin (HB)

Women: 12.5 | Men: 13.0

Donation Interval

Women: 90days | Men: 60days

Preparation

Must have eaten a meal and had plenty of fluids before arriving.

Recent Procedures

No new tattoos or piercings in the last 4 months.

KEY DEFERRAL PERIODS & EXCLUSIONS

  • *Infection/Flu:* Must wait until symptoms are gone (typically *24 hours* to *1 week*).
  • *Major Surgery/Childbirth:* Wait period usually *6 months*.
  • *High-Risk Travel (e.g., Malaria):* Must wait *3 months* after returning from an endemic area.
  • *Medication:* Deferral required for certain drugs (e.g., specific antibiotics or acne treatments).
  • *Permanent Deferral:* For conditions like HIV, Hepatitis B/C, or uncontrolled severe cardiac disease.
